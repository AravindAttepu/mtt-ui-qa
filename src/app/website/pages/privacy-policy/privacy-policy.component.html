1. Introduction & Scope

Sadisha Foundation ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide while using our website (sadisha.org) and our services, in accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 (SPDI Rules).

By accessing our website or donating to our causes, you signify your agreement to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

  • Personal Identification: Name, age, gender, and contact details (email, phone number, physical address) when you register for programs or contact us.
  • Sensitive Personal Data (For Program Registration): Aadhaar numbers (collected for student identification in talent tests), student photographs, and academic history.
  • Donor Information: PAN card details (necessary for issuing 80G tax receipts) and contribution history.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and website usage statistics via cookies and analytics.

3. How the Information is Used

We use the information we collect for the following non-commercial purposes:

  • To process student registrations for the Mathematics Talent Test and GATE coaching initiatives.
  • To issue and deliver 80G tax-exemption receipts for your donations.
  • To generate hall tickets, certificates, and academic resources for students.
  • To communicate impact reports, newsletters, and upcoming volunteer opportunities.
  • To maintain internal records for audit and compliance requirements.

4. Donation & Payment Data

We currently accept contributions through Direct Bank Transfers (NEFT/IMPS), UPI Transfers (via secure QR codes), and Benevity (for corporate giving). Sadisha Foundation does not use payment aggregators like Razorpay or net banking gateways at this time. We do not store your banking credentials or sensitive financial data on our servers. All international contributions via Benevity are handled through their secure, PCI-compliant infrastructure.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyze website traffic via Google Analytics. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect certain features of the website.

6. Data Protection & Security

We implement reasonable physical, electronic, and managerial security procedures to safeguard your data. Our website uses SSL/HTTPS encryption to protect information during transmission. Access to sensitive personal data is restricted to authorized personnel on a "need-to-know" basis for operational fulfillment.

7. Data Sharing & Disclosure Policy

We have a strict policy against selling, trading, or renting your personal information to third parties.

Information is shared only under the following conditions:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operations (e.g., web hosting) under confidentiality agreements.
  • To comply with regulatory obligations, such as reporting donor details to the Income Tax Department.
  • If required by law or a valid government request (e.g., NITI Aayog).

8. User Consent & Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to review and update the personal information you have shared with us.
  • The right to withdraw consent for data processing (noting that this may impact service delivery or 80G receipt issuance).
  • The right to request deletion from our communication/mailing lists.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or laws. Significant updates will be highlighted on this page.
