Mathematics Talent Test (MTT)

Discovering Brilliance, Guaranteeing Success

Our flagship initiative since 2012, identifying raw mathematical aptitude in government school students (Class 10). The MTT isn't just an exam—it's a movement to reignite the love for mathematics among rural school children. We reach out to thousands of students across multiple districts in Telangana.

Why Math? We identified that fear of mathematics is a primary reason for school dropouts and poor academic confidence. MTT is designed to be conceptual and encouraging.

  • Annual Assessment: Conducted across multiple districts to find the top 1% students.
  • Full Scholarship: Providing tuition fees and books for +2 education.
  • Dedicated Mentorship: Personal guidance for students to navigate their academic journey.

Residential GATE Coaching

Empowering Women, Elevating the Nation

For women CSE graduates from underprivileged backgrounds, cracking GATE is the gateway to IITs and PSUs. But quality coaching costs ₹1-2 lakhs—an impossible dream for most. Sadisha Foundation bridges this gap.

Breaking Barriers, Building Engineers: Our volunteer mentors, many of whom are IIT alumni or GATE toppers themselves, provide not just technical coaching but also psychosocial support and career counseling. We believe that when you educate a woman engineer, you empower a generation.

  • ₹5,000 Stipend: Monthly financial support to ensure focus on studies.
  • Free Accommodation: All-inclusive residential facility in Karimnagar.
  • Expert Guidance: Technical coaching by alumni from premier IITs and NITs.
