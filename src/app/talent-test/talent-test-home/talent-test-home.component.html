The Talent-Opportunity Gap

Talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. In rural India, thousands of brilliant minds in government schools lack the resources and guidance to compete on a global stage.

Our Core Focus Areas:
  • Government High Schools
  • Telangana Model Schools

The Process

Empowering Class 10 students through structured support.

01. Identification

A dedicated Mathematics Talent Test conducted strictly for Class 10 students in government schools.

02. Selection

Full scholarship for Intermediate (+2) education, covering tuition, books, and essential resources.

03. Support

Continuous mentorship and career guidance to help students transition into professional institutes.

Eligibility Criteria

Students from only ZP, Model, Gurukul (TGSWREIS) or Kasturba (KGBV) Schools in class 10th are eligible to register.

Exam Structure

  • Open Book: Textbooks and notes are permitted.
  • Format: 10 challenging problems.
  • Duration: 60 minutes.
  • Strategy: Relative scoring based on problem difficulty.
