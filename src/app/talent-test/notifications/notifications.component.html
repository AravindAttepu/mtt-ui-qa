Eligibility Criteria

Only students from ZP, Model, Gurukul (TGSWREIS) or Kasturba (KGBV) schools in class 10th are eligible. If your school is not listed, call 9550895968 or email admin@sadisha.org.

Exam Structure

  • Open Book: Textbooks and notes permitted.
  • Duration: 60 minutes | 10 Problems.
  • Security: Mobile phones & calculators strictly prohibited.
  • Mandatory: Aadhaar card & Hall-Ticket required.

Scoring Strategy

Marks are relative based on problem difficulty levels:

  • Easy problems: ~1.12 marks
  • Complex problems: ~1.99 marks

Prize Structure

Up to 80 students receive full scholarship for Intermediate (+2) including hostel fees. Top performing schools receive mementos.
